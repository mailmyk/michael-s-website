Places to Visit

Touring Turkey is an amazing experience, revealing fresh cultures and hidden gems with every curious step. There are so many incredible destinations to explore, from majestic mountain ranges in Antalya and roaring waterfalls in Bodum, to sun-drenched pristine beaches in Mamaris and ancient forests in Belgrad.

Antalya

Antalya is a breathtakingly beautiful city located in southern Turkey that is so much more than just its reputation of being a beach vacationer's paradise! An exquisite mix of a vibrant cityscape and natural beauty, it offers activities to please everyone. There are plenty of luxury resorts dotted along the coastline with private beaches and turquoise seas, but many visitors are drawn to the ancient sites such as Hadrian’s Gate, Kaleiçi (old quarter) and the iconic Düden Waterfalls. Nature lovers rejoice with nature parks and reserves around every corner offering hikes, jeep safaris, hot air balloon rides over secluded valleys or even paragliding. With some of the best nightlife in Turkey too, as well as excellent shopping opportunities, there couldn't be anywhere better to go on an exciting holiday!

Instanbul

Istanbul is an incredible destination for any traveler! From the stunning architecture of its mosques to its bustling bazaars, there's something for everyone in this magical city. Take a stroll along the shores of the Bosphorus Strait and enjoy amazing views of the skyline. Enjoy traditional Turkish cuisine in one of hundreds of amazing cafes scattered throughout the city. You can also take advantage of world-class shopping and nightlife, as well as attractions like Hagia Sophia and Topkapi Palace! Istanbul provides a unique cultural experience that can't be found anywhere else - make sure to put it on your bucket list today!

Bodrum

Bodrum is an exciting and vibrant destination that should not be missed! Located along the Aegean coast of Turkey, its beautiful seaside setting looks out on crystal clear waters that invite exploration. The charming town bustles with ancient marinas, historical ruins like the Mausoleum of Halicarnassus - one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World and plenty of tasty traditional cuisine. Enjoy relaxing days exploring nearby beaches, or hopping from island to island like Rabbit Island, Gümüslük Bay, Yalıçiftlik and Palamutbükü. Get your adrenaline pumping at some famous windsurfing spots around Kumburnu with over 8km beach strip for action-packed activities including kiteboarding, sailing, waterparks, and much more! Bodrum has so much to offer and is sure to excite you with its perfect combination of history and relaxation!
